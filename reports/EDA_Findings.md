# EDA Key Findings — Bluestock Fintech MF Capstone

1. **Small Cap funds outperform** — SBI Small Cap delivered 23.39% 
   3-year CAGR, highest among all 40 schemes analysed.

2. **SIP inflows hit all-time high** — Monthly SIP inflows reached 
   ₹31,002 Cr in Dec 2025, showing 3x growth since Jan 2022.

3. **T30 cities dominate** — 66.3% of all investments come from 
   Top 30 cities, while B30 cities contribute 33.7%.

4. **26-35 age group most active** — This group made 13,463 
   transactions, the highest among all age groups.

5. **UPI is preferred payment mode** — Over 55% of transactions 
   use UPI, reflecting India's digital payment adoption.

6. **Equity funds have higher expense ratios** — Average expense 
   ratio for Equity funds is 1.34% vs 0.67% for Debt funds.

7. **SBI dominates AUM** — SBI Mutual Fund manages ₹12.5 lakh crore,
   the largest AUM among all fund houses.

8. **High NAV correlation among Large Cap funds** — Large Cap funds 
   show 0.85+ correlation, meaning they move together with the market.

9. **92% investors are KYC verified** — Only 8% pending, showing 
   strong compliance in the investor base.

10. **Folio count doubled** — Industry folios grew from 13.26 crore 
    in Jan 2022 to 26.12 crore in Dec 2025 — 2x growth in 4 years!
